NG-CDF Masinga Bursary Program

The NG-CDF Masinga Bursary Program is designed to support students from financially disadvantaged backgrounds by providing financial assistance for secondary school, college, and university education.

Who Can Apply?

Students enrolled in recognized Secondary schools, Colleges, or Universities.

Applicants must be voter residents of Masinga Constituency.

Application Requirements

  • Eligibility: STRICT MASINGA CONSTITUENCY VOTER RESIDENTS ONLY.
  • Secondary Levels: HIGH SCHOOL, SPECIAL SCHOOLS, AND SENIOR SECONDARY MUST SUBMIT HARD-COPY APPLICATIONS IN PERSON (GUIDELINES FOR PHYSICAL APPLICATIONS WILL BE ISSUED IN DUE COURSE).
  • Tertiary Levels (online form): CERTIFICATE, DIPLOMA, ARTISAN, DEGREE.
  • Mandatory Document for All: SIGNED AND STAMPED CHIEF/SUB-CHIEF LETTER.
  • UPLOAD A PHOTO OF YOUR ID OR BIRTH CERTIFICATE
  • First-Year Students: UPLOAD YOUR ADMISSION LETTER.
  • Continuing Students: UPLOAD YOUR LATEST ACADEMIC TRANSCRIPT.
  • Disability: UPLOAD OFFICIAL DISABILITY SUPPORTING DOCUMENT.
  • Single-Parent Household: UPLOAD PROOF OF SINGLE PARENTHOOD STATUS (SIGNED AND STAMPED BY CHIEF/SUB-CHIEF).
  • Orphan (parent deceased): UPLOAD THE PARENT'S DEATH CERTIFICATE AND PROOF YOU ARE THEIR CHILD/SIBLING (E.G., BIRTH CERTIFICATE OR AFFIDAVIT).
  • Important: ANY FALSE INFORMATION OR NON-RESIDENCY LEADS TO DISQUALIFICATION.
  • For assistance contact: 0113781366 or 0722759218
